11  This package implements optimization by simulated annealing. Consult:

14          Implementation of the basic simulated annealing algorithms;

18  Simulated Annealing is a meta-heuristic designed for optimization of functions. 
19  It tries to mimic the way that atoms settle in crystal structures of metals. By 
20  slowly cooling the metal, atoms settle in a position of low energy -- thus, it 
21  is a natural optimization method. 
22   
23  Two kinds of optimizer are implemented here. The continuous version of the 
24  algorithm can be used for optimization of continuous objective functions; the
